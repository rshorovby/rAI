# Source

**URL:** https://www.feeltennis.net/experience-more-time/

## Key claims

1. Rec ball flight ~1.5–2s; calm players perceive more time units.
2. Beginner: 1 clear focus after bounce → rush; advanced: 3–5 → choose contact.
3. Drills: count fast bounce→contact или opponent contact→own contact.
4. 2–3 мин часто дают «больше времени»; не в матче под стрессом.
5. Связь Gallwey bounce-hit; focus на flights between events.
