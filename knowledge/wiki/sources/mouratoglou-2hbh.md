# Source · Mouratoglou 2HBH guide

**URL:** https://www.mouratoglou.com/en/conseils-coaching/coaching-corner/tennis-technique/mastering-the-two-handed-backhand-stroke-the-guide/

## Key claims

1. Grip RH: dominant (bottom) **Continental**; non-dom (top) **Eastern / Semi-Western** — non-dom = power + topspin, dominant = control/face.
2. Ready: athletic stance; marked **shoulder rotation** / racket back = coil.
3. Power vs 1HBH: сильнее зависит от **weight transfer** back→front + legs/trunk, не от «руки».
4. Contact: waist height, slightly in front; synergy обеих рук.
5. Finish: continue forward; non-dom arm often over dominant shoulder.
6. Variants: topspin/lifted (в тексте ошибочно «backspin»), two-hand slice, flat.
7. Errors: insufficient shoulder turn; arms-only; late contact.

## Caveats

Academy marketing blog, не peer-reviewed.  
Grip non-dom: Eastern **или** SW — шире, чем строгий FT Eastern-only ([[feeltennis-grips]]).
