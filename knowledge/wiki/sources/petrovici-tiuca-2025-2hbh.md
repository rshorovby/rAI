# Source · Petrovici & Tiuca 2025

**Authors:** G. A. Petrovici, I. C. Tiuca  
**Journal:** Analele Universității din Oradea, Fascicula Educație Fizică și Sport, XXXV, 79–84 (2025)  
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.30892/AUOFEFS.2025.09  
**PDF:** https://www.fefs.uoradea.ro/Fascicula_Educatie_Fizica_si_Sport/2025/AUOFEFS.2025.09.pdf

## Key claims

1. 2HBH: стабильность на контакте, topspin, контроль, меньше unilateral load vs 1HBH.
2. Grip predominates: dominant **Continental** + non-dom **Eastern/SW**; non-dom = primary force engine (pull/rotate/accelerate); dominant stabilizes face.
3. Kinetic chain: ground → legs → pelvis/trunk → shoulders → arms → racket; prep stores elastic tension (unit turn).
4. Phases: prep (unit turn) → acceleration (legs+pelvis; non-dom motor) → impact ahead near CoM → follow-through (energy dissipation + recovery).
5. Late / too-close contact → плохой transfer + нагрузка на shoulder/elbow.
6. Stances: square = linear weight transfer; semi-open/open = tempo adaptation.
7. Teaching: progressive (stationary → moving → situational); individualize by anthropometrics; tech (IMU/mocap) optional.
8. Health: bilateral load distribution; core/pelvis involvement; caveat — много lab studies, мало match/longitudinal.
