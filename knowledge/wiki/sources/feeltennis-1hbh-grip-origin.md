## Key claims

1. Table tennis flip (back of hand to ball) ≠ tennis — силы больше, нужен stable wrist.
2. Tennis 1HBH: turn arm ~90°, palm still to ball, fingers ~45° up; back of hand ~90° from ball.
3. Не «common sense» flip.
